## Case Study 1: Danny's Diner

## Problem: Understanding customer behavior and spending patterns.

## Key Insights

1. Identifying popular items helps the restaurant focus on what drives most orders.
- This allows better inventory planning and menu optimization.

2. Customer-level analysis reveals different preferences.
- Knowing what each customer likes enables personalized recommendations.

3. Membership behavior provides valuable signals.
- Understanding when and why customers join can help improve membership strategies.

4. Targeted marketing becomes more effective with data.
- Businesses can send customized messages based on customer preferences and increase conversions.
